About Our Store


Littlebelle is a women's accessories brand founded on January 15, 2024. It was founded with the aim of providing elegant, fashionable, and affordable accessories for women who want to add a special touch to their appearance. Since its inception, Littlebelle has focused on modern designs with high-quality materials, as well as choices that can be worn for various occasions, from everyday to formal events. Each accessory collection released by Littlebelle is carefully selected to create an elegant and luxurious impression, while still combining comfort and practicality. This brand aims to empower women to be more confident through the accessories they wear.
